What is this thing?

This app is a wrapper for the monthly Hacker News' Who is hiring? thread, with the job posts sorted by location. It is not affiliated with HN. @HR: if you want your job offers to show up here you have to submit them to the original thread.

Why did you build this, and who are you?

I'm a new grad, back from 7+ months of motorbiking solo through SE Asia. Being the travel addict that I am, as I was looking for a job as a SDE I started to wander where in the world I wanted to go next. Hacker News' Who is hiring? was obviously my go-to place, but the UI is so not pretty, right?

Anyhow, I'm still looking! Here's my resume; feel free to drop me an email. Also, I'd love to have some general feedback on the website. You can find my GPG key here.

Is the code on Github?

Sure! For both the website and the database. Check it out here.

Is there an API?

Not yet, but I'm working on it :)
In the meantime, you can easily build your own local Sqlite copy of the database; check out the README for further instructions.

How frequently is the database updated?

Starting next month (May) the site will probably auto-update live every 15 minutes or something from the moment the new post hits the front page. I have most of the code ready but I still need to write tests. I was getting a lot of stuff done, and then Tinder. Ouch.

Why is xyz city not showing up?

To speed up the db indexing I decided to start with a fairly small set of cities (~1k) and then to manually augment the city table every time a post didn't match any city (yeah that was a pretty boring monkey job); therefore it can happen that a fairly small city is not in the db yet. Drop me an email and I'll add it.

What is the stack behind wwh? ?

Python + Flask + SQLAlchemy, Nginx (http + reverse proxy cache), MySQL, Pure CSS hosted on my Digital Ocean VPS. Kudos to the excellent BeautifulSoup library for web scraping.
